Hi!

My name is Chenyu Zhang. I am an undergraduate student at the Johns Hopkins University. I was born in Nanjing, China, and I currently live in Baltimore, Maryland, USA. This portfolio webpage is written during my internship at Google in summer 2020.

At JHU, my majors are Applied Math, Computer Science, and Cognitive Science. I enjoy all three of my majors and I think they complement each other very well. Aside from school, I am part of the Blue Orchid Dance Club, where we practice trational Chineses dances and give performances at various events (which are, sadly, suspended due to COVID-19). I am also a research assistant at the Mind Brain Institute, and a software developer on the Otoglobal Health design team.

I believe that the perfect timeline is where you know what is going to happen tomorrow, but not what is going to happen next year, so that there is a balance between excitement and stableness. While I have not decided on exactly what to do with the rest of my life, I'm aiming toward somewhere in tech, a field that I enjoy the most. Like many, I adore the sunshine in California. And I believe that with my current or future skills, I will be able to make an impact.
